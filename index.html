About

Building systems
that compound.

Noah Carmichael is an executive, investor, publisher, and best-selling author. Managing Partner of Carmichael & Russ Capital. Publisher & Editor-in-Chief of The Black Executive Journal™. His work runs across private equity acquisition, capital deployment, media infrastructure ownership, and global capital formation.

He founded and led Esquire Marketing Group — a nationally recognized legal marketing firm with exclusive partnerships across major digital advertising networks. He authored A Lawyer's Guide to Marketing, a best-selling title that defined a vertical.

What he is building now is a different order of magnitude — acquiring and building a vertically integrated, globally oriented ecosystem that owns the infrastructure those markets run on. The systems. The media. The technology. The capital. Companies that come into the ecosystem don't just gain a partner. They gain the rails.
