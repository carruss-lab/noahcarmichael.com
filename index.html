About

Building systems
that compound.

Noah Carmichael is an American executive, investor, publisher, and best-selling author. His work sits at the intersection of private equity-style acquisition strategy, operator-led capital deployment, media infrastructure ownership, and long-term global capital formation. He is Managing Partner of Carmichael & Russ Capital and Publisher & Editor-in-Chief of The Black Executive Journal™.

Before building his current platform, Noah founded and led Esquire Marketing Group — a specialized marketing agency serving the legal services sector, scaled into an award-winning, nationally recognized firm with exclusive partnerships across major digital advertising networks. He authored A Lawyer's Guide to Marketing, a best-selling title that defined a vertical.

Today the work is larger in scope — building and acquiring a vertically integrated, globally oriented ecosystem of companies designed to own the infrastructure those markets run on: the systems, the media, the technology, the capital, and the people. Not to participate in markets. To own the rails they run on.
