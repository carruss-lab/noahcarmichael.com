The Premise

Across markets and across decades,
one pattern appears again and again.

Coordinated capital changes outcomes.
  • Infrastructure is built.
  • Innovation ecosystems emerge.
  • Long-term investment replaces fragmented flows.

These results were not accidental. They were structured.

This project began by studying those precedents — not to compete with them, but to learn from them. To identify the architecture behind successful capital formation and present it in a clear, structured framework.

The result is a two-part release designed for both decision-makers and operators.

Why Two Editions

Different audiences require different depths.

Some readers need the thesis — concise, strategic, and focused. Others need the framework — detailed, structured, and implementable. So the release includes both.

Executive Edition

The Strategic Brief

Common Questions

Before you secure your edition

Reservations are fulfilled in the order received during the founding window.

When will I be charged?

Immediately, at checkout. This is a direct reservation — not a pledge. Your card is charged the moment you confirm, and you'll receive a Stripe receipt by email with your order details.

What's the difference between the Executive and Standard editions?

The Executive Edition is a concise strategic brief — the thesis distilled for decision-makers who want the architecture and precedents. The Standard Edition is the full framework — the complete expansion into structure, design, and implementation, written for operators putting the ideas into practice. They're designed to complement each other.

When will physical copies ship?

Paperback editions are expected to ship within 6 weeks of the founding release closing. Signed and collector editions, which require signing and hand-numbering, ship within 8–10 weeks. Institutional orders coordinate shipping directly.

Is shipping included? Where do you ship?

Shipping is included worldwide on every physical tier — no add-on at checkout, no surprise fees. The price you see is the price you pay. Orders ship tracked from our distribution partner's print and fulfillment network, with delivery to virtually any country.

Who is publishing and distributing the book?

The book is published by Glidepath Media, a division of Carmichael & Russ Capital. Global distribution is handled through IngramSpark, the independent publishing platform operated by Lightning Source LLC. This places the book in Ingram's worldwide trade catalogue — available to bookstores, libraries, and online retailers across the United States, United Kingdom, European Union, Canada, Australia, and more than 40,000 retailers globally.

IngramSpark® is a service of Lightning Source LLC. © Copyright Lightning Source LLC. All Rights Reserved. Used here for distribution attribution.

What is the Black Executive Journal, and how do I access it?

The Black Executive Journal is a subscription publication delivering ongoing executive-level writing that builds on the thesis — market analysis, precedent case studies, and structural frameworks released on a regular cadence. It is included with Insider Access (3 months), Collector Box Set (6 months), and both Partner tiers (12 months).

How you'll receive access: immediately after checkout, you'll receive two emails — your Stripe payment receipt, and a separate welcome message from the Black Executive Journal with your personalized access link and first issue. The link works across any device, no app required, and your subscription activates the moment payment is confirmed. If you don't see the welcome email within 15 minutes, check your spam folder or email us directly.

How do digital copies get delivered?

On launch day, every digital-inclusive tier receives a download link at the email used at checkout. Files are DRM-free PDF and EPUB — readable on any device, no app required, yours to keep.

Is the founding supporter recognition public?

Your name appears in the acknowledgments section of the printed editions and on the founding supporters page of the project website. If you'd prefer to remain anonymous or use an initial/pseudonym, reply to your receipt and we'll arrange it before print.

Can I buy a single edition after the founding release closes?

Yes — individual editions open to the public in approximately November 2026. Until then, both editions are available exclusively through this founding window. Every public copy printed in November carries the names of founding supporters in its acknowledgments, so if you want your name in the permanent record of the release, the founding window is the only opportunity.

Refund policy?

Full refund on request for any reason until books are sent to the printer (roughly 4 weeks after the founding release closes). After that, refunds are reviewed case by case. Reply to your Stripe receipt to request.

How can institutions arrange a bulk or custom order?

For orders larger than the $7,500 Strategic tier, custom sponsorship, or co-branded institutional editions, reach out directly at noah@noahcarmichael.com.
